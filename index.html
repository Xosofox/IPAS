IPAS - Ingress Portal Attack Simulator

This simulator lets you plan an attack on an enemy portal. It tries to simulate the damage of your bursters, depending on the position and level

As there are some game mechanisms not fully analysed yet, the plan is that you can switch between different calculation methods regarding shield influence and damage reduction due to distance

Currently, distance influence is quadratic, not linear
